CrIS Suomi NPP#

These products are retrieved from CrIS Suomi NPP measurements via the Optimal estimation method.

Standard Products#

  • Air Temperature (TATM) - Air temperature at different pressure levels / altitudes

  • Ammonia (NH3) - Vertical distribution of ammonia

  • Carbon monoxide (CO) - Vertical distribution of carbon monoxide

  • Methane (CH4) - Vertical distribution of methane

  • Ozone (O3) - Vertical distribution of ozone

  • Peroxyacetyl Nitrate (PAN) - Vertical distribution of peroxyacetyl nitrate.

  • Semi-heavy water vapor (HDO) - Vertical distribution of semi-heavy water vapor

  • Water vapor (H2O) - Vertical distribution of water vapor

Summary Products#

  • Ammonia (NH3) - Vertically integrated total column

  • Carbon monoxide (CO) - Column-averaged dry air mixing ratio from the surface to the Top of the Atmosphere (TOA), also known as XCO

  • Methane (CH4) - Column-averaged dry air mixing ratio for the partial column between 826 hPa and the Top of the Atmosphere (TOA), also known as free tropospheric XCH4

  • Ozone (O3) - Vertically integrated total column and tropospheric column

  • Peroxyacetyl Nitrate (PAN) - Column-averaged dry air mixing ratio for the partial column between 826 hPa and 215 hPa, also known as free tropospheric XPAN

  • Semi-heavy water vapor (HDO / Delta-D) - Partial column between 850 and 250 hPa, expressed in parts per thousand relative to the deuterium content of ocean waters (also known as Delta-D)
