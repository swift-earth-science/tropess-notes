AIRS + OMI | Forward Stream | Standard#

These products are generated from collocated AIRS and OMI measurements

AIRS Only#

Methane (CH4)#

Vertical distribution of methane (CH4), retrieved from AIRS measurements. Download from GES DISC.

Carbon monoxide (CO)#

Vertical distribution of carbon monoxide (CO), retrieved from AIRS measurements. Download from GES DISC.

Water vapor (H2O)#

Vertical distribution of water vapor (H2O), retrieved from AIRS measurements. Download from GES DISC.

Semi-heavy water vapor (HDO)#

Vertical distribution of semi-heavy water vapor (HDO), retrieved from AIRS measurements. Download from GES DISC.

Ammonia (NH3)#

Vertical distribution of ammonia (NH3), retrieved from AIRS measurements. Download from GES DISC.

Ozone (O3)#

Vertical distribution of ozone (O3), retrieved from AIRS measurements. Download from GES DISC.

Air temperature (TATM)#

Air temperature (TATM) at different pressure levels / altitudes, retrieved from AIRS measurements. Download from GES DISC.

OMI Only#

Ozone (O3)#

Vertical distribution of of ozone (O3), retrieved from OMI measurements. Download from GES DISC.

AIRS + OMI Joint#

Ozone (O3)#

Vertical distribution of of ozone (O3), retrieved from combined AIRS and OMI measurements, as opposed to AIRS Only and OMI Only ozone. Download from GES DISC.
